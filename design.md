# WeBond Design Style Guide

## Design Philosophy

### Color Palette
- **Primary**: Deep Ocean Blue (#1e3a8a) - Trust, reliability, professionalism
- **Secondary**: Warm Coral (#f97316) - Energy, warmth, human connection
- **Accent**: Soft Mint (#10b981) - Growth, harmony, success
- **Neutral**: Cool Gray (#64748b) - Balance, sophistication
- **Background**: Pure White (#ffffff) with subtle gray tones (#f8fafc)

### Typography
- **Display Font**: Inter Bold - Modern, clean, highly readable
- **Body Font**: Inter Regular - Consistent, professional, accessible
- **Accent Font**: Inter Medium - Emphasis without overwhelming

### Visual Language
- **Minimalist Approach**: Clean, uncluttered interfaces focusing on functionality
- **Human-Centered Design**: Warm, approachable aesthetics that build trust
- **Professional Trust**: Corporate-grade visual quality with personal warmth
- **Cultural Sensitivity**: Inclusive design respecting diverse backgrounds

## Visual Effects & Styling

### Used Libraries
- **Anime.js**: Smooth micro-interactions and state transitions
- **ECharts.js**: Data visualization for matching analytics and user insights
- **Splide.js**: Image carousels for success stories and cultural highlights
- **p5.js**: Interactive background patterns and creative coding elements
- **Pixi.js**: Advanced visual effects for premium user experience

### Animation & Effects
- **Subtle Fade-ins**: Content reveals with 200ms timing
- **Hover Transformations**: 3D tilt effects on cards and buttons
- **Gradient Flows**: Animated background gradients for visual interest
- **Particle Systems**: Floating connection dots symbolizing network effects
- **Smooth Transitions**: Page navigation with slide and fade combinations

### Header & Navigation Effects
- **Glass Morphism**: Semi-transparent navigation with backdrop blur
- **Dynamic Shadows**: Elevated elements with soft, realistic shadows
- **Color Cycling**: Subtle color transitions on interactive elements
- **Micro-interactions**: Button press animations and form field focus states

### Layout Principles
- **Grid System**: 12-column responsive grid for consistent alignment
- **Whitespace**: Generous spacing for breathing room and focus
- **Hierarchy**: Clear visual hierarchy through size, color, and positioning
- **Accessibility**: WCAG 2.1 AA compliance with 4.5:1 contrast ratios
