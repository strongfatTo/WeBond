How It Works

Getting started with WeBond is simple. Follow these steps to begin your cultural exchange journey and find the help you need.

1

Sign Up

Create your profile and complete verification. Choose whether you're seeking help or offering assistance.

2

Post or Browse

International students post tasks they need help with. Local residents browse available tasks in their area.

3

AI Matching

Our AI analyzes preferences, location, and availability to suggest the best matches for both parties.

4

Connect & Complete

Chat, arrange meetings, complete tasks, and build lasting friendships while learning about different cultures.
